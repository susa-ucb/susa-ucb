About our team

We are a group of undergraduates interested in acting as outreach to all students in statistics. Our group consists of a president, treasurer, secretary, external committee, and internal committe. The website is maintained by anyone on our team.

Duties

President

The president oversees all activites within our organization and approves of all decisions sent in and out of the team.

Treasurer

The treasurer oversees all financial flow in the organization and is the medium for all of our financial activites. Budgeting and reimbursements are handled by the treasurer.

Secretary

The secretary keeps records of all meetings and enforces attendance within the team. In addition, proposals for events are also sent by the secretary to other parties.

External Committee

The external committee is in charge of planning and organizing events that relate to getting speakers and anything that involves parties outside of SUSA.

Internal Committee

The internal committee is in charge of planning and organizing events that relate directly to our community of undergraduates. Activities include planning for study halls, socials, and creating materials for classes.

How to join us?

To join our team of officers, please apply during one of our recruitment seasons! We will be recruiting at the start of the fall and winter semesters. As an expection to the start of our organization, we will also be holding recruitment around the end of this spring semester, so be on the lookout for it!
